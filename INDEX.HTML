Rental Policies & Information

Rental Terms

  • Minimum rental duration: 24 hours.
  • Drivers must be at least 18 years old with a valid driving license.
  • Fuel charges apply if the vehicle is returned with less fuel than rented.

Insurance Coverage

  • All vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage.
  • Insurance covers damages due to accidents, theft, and third-party liability.
  • Customer is responsible for any fines or violations during the rental period.

Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the rental start time.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours will incur a 20% cancellation fee.
  • No-shows will be charged 50% of the rental amount.
